Shauna B

Sex education specialist

As a journalist specializing in sex education, Shauna has seen all too often what can happen when people don’t receive adequate sex education. She hopes that We Are Beautiful can help young people and adults gain a better understanding about bodies and realize just how beautiful they truly are.

Shauna runs the site Stumbling on Sexuality and writes for different publications about sexuality, women’s health, and feminist politics. You can follow her on Twitter for all things feminism and internet.

There could’t be more truth in the words We Are Beautiful.
