About Quatsch und Unfug

Quatsch und Unfug ÖU is a for-profit company that is run as a non-profit company. Setting up non-profits is hard and expensive, so we start the easy way. We promise to be responsible and tranparent with your support! But, if someone feel strongly that we should be a non-profit and would like to sponsor this change, please contact us.

We plan to only raise enough funds to cover our costs for operating the site, creating 3D models and providing us the tools to do this. We will never take dividends or otherwise profit from operating this site.

However, we do have costs to take pictures of human models, sign modeling release contracts, make 3D models, provide human models with one printed 3D model of their own genitalia (as a thank you) and a host of other small costs. We are also constantly investing in technology to create models of inceasing complexity and with greater resolution.

If you appreciate what we do with this site, we would appreciate a small support payment. Since we are a for profit company masquerading as a non-profit, we cannot really call them donations. Also, these payments are also not tax deductible. But they are like donations in that you get nothing in return for them, except our undying eternal gratitude. \ø/
