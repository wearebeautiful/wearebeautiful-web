Our Company

Quatsch und Unfug ÖU is a for-profit company that is run as a non-profit company. Setting up non-profits is hard and expensive, so we start the easy way. We promise to be responsible and tranparent with your support! But, if someone feel strongly that we should be a non-profit and would like to sponsor this change, please contact us.

We plan to only raise enough funds to cover our costs for operating the site, creating 3D models and providing us the tools to do this. We will never take dividends or otherwise profit from operating this site.

However, we do have costs to take pictures of human models, sign modeling release contracts, make 3D models, provide human models with one printed 3D model of their own genitalia (as a thank you) and a host of other small costs.

If you appreciate what we do with this site, we would appreciate a small support payment. Since we are a for profit company masquerading as a non-profit, we cannot really call them donations. Also, these payments are also not tax deductible.

Each year we raise funds to cover our operational cost for the next year. Once our budget has been met, we will redirect visitors to donate to other sex-positive non-profits rathen than to us.
