Come for the bits, stay for the education.

We create 3D models of human genitals with the aim of breaking taboos, educating at a global level, and improving our users self body images. We aim to enable educators to do the same by making the 3D models freely available for viewing online and for downloading/printing under the CC0 license.

Our photographer take photos of volunteers and process these photos to produce 3D models of their genitals and other body parts. These 3D models freely available for viewing on this site and for downloading so that you may print them on your own 3D printer.

This project exists because we feel that there is a lack of educational material that shows what real people look like. We feel that a lot of people have very little appreciation for if their own genitals look normal. We hope to show that there is no normal and that we humans are all beautiful.
